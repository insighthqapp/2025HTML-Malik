Headline H3 - COMING SOON: Customizable “reply-to field” for sending product updates through HubSpot

With a planned delivery next week, LaunchNotes projects using HubSpot will be able to choose a different reply-to address for your LaunchNotes notification emails in HubSpot, giving you more flexibility and control over how replies are managed.

What’s New:

  • Add a reply-to address that’s separate from the sending address.
  • Improved customer experience with replies direct to the appropriate team.
  • Improved management of email response flows.
  • Get immediate visual feedback for your embedded content.

We’ve refined the HubSpot subscriber confirmation workflow to ensure a smoother experience for both new and existing subscribers.

New subscribers are immediately confirmed, and for new and returning subscribers a confirmation email is now sent via HubSpot. A clear "Check your inbox" page and a direct link to "Manage Subscription" for easy access.
